Delete Your Booh Brawlers Account

If you wish to permanently delete your Booh Brawlers account and all associated data, you can do so directly from within the app.

How to Delete Your Account

  • Open the Booh Brawlers app
  • Tap the Settings icon (gear symbol)
  • Scroll down and select Delete Account
  • Confirm your decision when prompted

This process is irreversible and will permanently remove all of your stored data, including:

  • Your email address
  • Username and password
  • Public wallet address
  • Progress and game data

If you are unable to access the app, you may request manual account deletion by emailing us at info@boohworld.com. Please allow up to 7 business days for processing.
